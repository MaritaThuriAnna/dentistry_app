What Is Smart Dental Office?

Smart Dental Office bridges the gap between dentists and patients by offering a seamless, AI-assisted dental management system. Whether you're analyzing X-rays or reviewing appointments, everything is just a click away.

Designed For Everyone

For Doctors

  • Manage patient records and history
  • Schedule appointments with ease
  • Upload X-rays and trigger AI analysis
  • Review and annotate diagnostic results

For Patients

  • Check upcoming and past appointments
  • Access personal data and history
  • View diagnostic images and doctor feedback
